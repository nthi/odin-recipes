Savory Oats

Description

If you are tired of sweets for breakfast, you might enjoy a savory take on classic oatmeal. Making savory oats in the microwave won't take any longer than usual, and you can tailor the recipe to your tastes. Makes one serving. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup oats (quick or old fashioned, but not steel cut)
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 teaspoon Better than Bouillon, any flavor (I like chicken or vegetable)
  • Optional seasoning ideas (mix, match, follow your heart)
    • One or two shakes of red pepper flakes
    • One garlic clove, chopped
    • Couple shakes of black pepper
    • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
    • Parmesan
    • Paprika
    • Etc.
